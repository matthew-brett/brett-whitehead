Richard Braginton Johns

Richard was born on 18 October 1862 (also FamilySearch record to Edmund Allen Johns and Clara Louisa Braginton).

See the Richard Braginton Johns Ancestry.com page for more suggestions.

He married Mary Sherwin in 1889. Mary probably died in 1898.

In 1902 he married Georgiana Lucy Deacon. Also see this FamilySearch page for Georgiana (needs login).

His household in Plymouth in the 1911 census is his wife, two children (see below) and a cook, two servants, a governess, a chauffeur and a stable boy.

His children from the 1911 census were:

  • Richard Allen Pendomer (actually Pendarves) Johns, age 18
  • George Edward (actually Edmund) Wolfe Johns, age 5

See link for Richard Allen Pendarves Johns.

George Edmund Wolfe Johns was born around December 1905, and died on 10 March 1920, aged 14, from measles and pneumonia.

Georgiana died on holiday with Richard in Italy, in 1937. The record appears in the free search for “Georgiana Johns” in the “Western Morning News” at https://www.britishnewspaperarchive.co.uk, but the full version quoted here is from this Wikitree page:

DIED IN ITALY

Wife of a Plymouth Magistrate. Many friends in Plymouth will learn with regret of the death abroad of Mrs Georgiana Lucy Johns, wife of Mr. R. B. Johns solicitor and city magistrate, of Plymouth, who had resided at The Terrace, The Hoe. Mrs Johns had been on a holiday visit to Italy with Mr. Johns, and death occurred at Menaggio, Italy, after short illness.

Her probate benefits her husband, and Flora Georgiana Deacon, the daughter from her first marriage (see Wikitree link above).

Richard himself died on 22 July 1952, leaving his £82940 to William Ernest Blakeney solicitor, and his step-daughter Flora Georgiana Deacon.

Flora died in 1970 in Plymouth, at the age of 75 or 76.
