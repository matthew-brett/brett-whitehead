Brett-Whitehead family history

Langford - Braginton

Henry Albert Langford married Clara Louisa Johns (née Braginton) in 1866, in Plymouth (BMD record).

The couple probably had two children:

  • Albert Leonard (1868-1879) (see below).
  • Helena Emmeline (1872-1949).

Albert Leonard Langford

Apart from Helena Emmeline, there was only one other Langford born in Plymouth between 1864 and 1890 - Albert Leonard Langford, in 1868 (BMD record).

Albert Leonard Langford died at age 11, in 1879, soon after his father, after “accidentally falling over the side of a quarry”.
