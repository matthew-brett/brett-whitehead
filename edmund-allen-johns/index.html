Brett-Whitehead family history

Edmund Allen Johns

Edmund Allen was born on 31 May 1865 to Edmund Allen Johns and Clara Louisa Braginton.

See also BMD birth records.

He was born six months months after the death of his father, also Edmund Allen, who died on 2 December 1864 (see above).

There is only one marriage for an Edmund Johns in a search from 1880 to 1929. The entry is for Bideford, in 1896, and the spouse’s name was probably Annie Hooper Mounce, by further search for other marriages in Bideford in the same period.

There is a 1930 probate entry for Edmond Allen:

JOHNS Edmond Allen of Penhallam Great Torrington Devonshire died 26 November 1929 Administration London 31 January to Richard Braginton Johns solicitor. Effects £1747 7s 9d.

England & Wales, National Probate Calendar (Index of Wills and Administrations), 1858-1966 - Ancestry.com - 1,1904::0 - 1,1904::5409983

It’s difficult to know whether his probate means he had no surviving wife or children.
