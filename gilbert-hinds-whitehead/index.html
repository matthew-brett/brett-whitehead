Brett-Whitehead family history

Gilbert Hinds Whitehead

According to a Peerage.com page on Sir James Whitehead, Gilbert Hinds Whitehead was the fifth child (and third son) of Sir James Whitehead, 1st Baronet and Mercy Matilda Hinds.

He was born 26th May 1866, and married Helena Emmeline Langford in 1901.

He died on 18th December 1908, age 42.
