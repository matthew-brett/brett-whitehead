Brett-Whitehead family history

Ellen Maccarmick Johns

Ellen was born in 1859 to Edmund Allen Johns and Clara Louisa Braginton.

See Ellen’s MyHeritage entry for more hints, and BMD birth records.

She was living with her mother as of the 1881 census, and married George Stawell that year.

In the 1891 census, she is living in Plymouth with her husband, a cook and a servant. For the 1901 census she is still living with her husband, and two different servants, but now in Great Torrington, Devon. For 1911, she is still in Great Torrington with her husband, and two new servants.

The couple don’t appear to have had any children; there are no “Stawell” births in Devon registered and recorded in FreeBMD.

Ellen died on 11 November 1938, leaving her £12,626 estate to a solicitor and foreign exchange broker.
