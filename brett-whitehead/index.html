Brett - Whitehead

Note for the family of John Brett (b 1905) and (Margaret) Joy Whitehead.

They married on 5 May 1928. See also BMD record.

John and Joy had three children:

  • Sue Oliver (Malta, 24 May 1929 — 21 March 2021).
  • Jenifer Brooke-Smith (Portsmouth, September 1931 - 9 September 2023).
  • Martin Brett (Woolwich 6 January 1939).
