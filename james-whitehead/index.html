James Whitehead

This page is for Sir James Whitehead, 1st Baronet.

James Whitehead was born in Bramhaw, Yorkshire on 2 March 1834.

See James Whitehead’s biography in Liberal History. (thanks to John Oliver for pointing me to this article).

Also see this Peerage.com page on Sir James Whitehead. See the Whitehead grave for some dates.

For more and earlier Whitehead family history see the Whitehead extract from Burke’s peerage

He married Mercy Matilda Hinds in 1860.

James died on 20 October 1917.
