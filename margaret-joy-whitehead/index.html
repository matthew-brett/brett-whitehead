Brett-Whitehead family history

Margaret Joy Whitehead

Margaret Joy went by “Joy”.

Her parents were Gilbert Hinds Whitehead and Helena Emmeline Whitehead.

Margaret Joy was born on 5 May 1928 (see Whitehead extract from Burke’s peerage).

She married John Brett in 1928.

John died in 1941. Joy married to Robert Clement Giles in 1965 (see Burke’s reference above).
