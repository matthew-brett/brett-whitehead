Margaret Joy Whitehead

Margaret Joy went by “Joy”.

Joy was born on 12 June 1905 (see BMD records for birth and death).

Her parents were Gilbert Hinds Whitehead and Helena Emmeline Whitehead.

She married John Brett in 1928, and had three children.

John died in 1941. Joy married Robert Clement Giles in 1965 (see Whitehead extract from Burke’s peerage). Robert Clement Giles was a staff officer in intelligence in Hong Kong when it fell to the Japanese in 1941, and was a prisoner of war until 1945.

Joy died in 1981 (BMD record).
