Brett-Whitehead family history

Alice Jane Braginton

Alice Jane’s birth record is was probably born in 1846 (BMD record).

This page on Braginton family history has her baptism date as 1845, and this Genenet page says she was baptised on 22nd December 1845.

Alice Jane married Samuel Henry Farwell Roe in 1890.

She died on 28 October 1929 (BMD record).

Alice Jane’s probate is:

ROE Alice Jane of St Pinnock Rectory near Liskeard Cornwall (wife of the reverend Samuel Henry Farwell Roe) died 23 October 1929 Probate London 23 December to the said reverend Samuel Henry Farwell Roe clerk and Helena Emmeline Whitehead widow. Effects £6539 5s. 5d.
